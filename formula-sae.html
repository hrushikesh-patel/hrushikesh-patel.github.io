Overview

As a member of Concordia Formula SAE, I contributed to multiple vehicle subsystems with a focus on performance-driven design. My work spanned aerodynamics, suspension and damper understanding, and vehicle dynamics, combining theoretical analysis with manufacturing and practical implementation.

Aerodynamics

  • Contributed to aerodynamic concept development to improve downforce and airflow consistency
  • Analyzed aerodynamic trade-offs with vehicle mass, balance, and packaging constraints
  • Considered manufacturability and structural integration of aerodynamic components
  • Worked closely with vehicle dynamics considerations to ensure balanced performance

Suspension & Dampers

  • Assisted in suspension geometry development with emphasis on handling and adjustability
  • Studied damper force–velocity characteristics and their influence on ride, grip, and transient response
  • Analyzed the role of damping in controlling oscillations and maintaining tire contact
  • Developed a strong understanding of why dampers are critical yet often overlooked performance components

Vehicle Dynamics

  • Applied vehicle dynamics principles to understand load transfer and handling balance
  • Linked suspension and aerodynamic decisions to overall vehicle performance
  • Supported setup discussions using theoretical and practical understanding
  • Focused on performance optimization rather than purely component-level design

Tools & Skills Used

  • SolidWorks
  • Hand calculations and theoretical analysis
  • Manufacturing and assembly collaboration
  • Team-based design decision making
